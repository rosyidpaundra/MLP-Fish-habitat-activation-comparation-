This repository contains the official QGIS Processing Toolbox scripts for replicating our comparative study on Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) activation functions (**Tanh, ReLU, and Sigmoid**) for Species Distribution Modeling (SDM) in the Fisheries Management Area of the Republic of Indonesia (**WPP NRI 573 / FMA 573**).

---

## 📌 Research Background & Novelty

Most global literature utilizes a single activation function for marine neural networks without empirical local justification. This research fills that gap by providing a sensitivity analysis of how non-linear mathematical behaviors (like vanishing or dying gradients) influence spatial prediction accuracy within highly dynamic upwelling zones.
